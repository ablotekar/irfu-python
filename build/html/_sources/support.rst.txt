=======
Support
=======

The easiest way to get help with the project is to join the ``#pyrfu``
channel on Slack_. We hang out there and you can get real-time help with
your projects.  The other good way is to open an issue on Gitlab_. You may 
also e-mail the `author <louis.richard@irfu.se>`_


.. _Slack: https://gitlab.com/lrich/irfu-python/-/tree/master/pyrfu
.. _Gitlab: https://gitlab.com/lrich/irfu-python/-/tree/master/pyrfu
